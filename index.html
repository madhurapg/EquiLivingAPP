About the website

The website will help identify neighborhoods at high risk of displacement and help the Affordable Housing Trust Fund (AHTF) committee to better allocate its funding sources in the neighborhoods need them most.

To identify displacement, this website works to show you three housing market indexes about Louisville: Initial Price Index, Price Change Index, Affordable Housing Development Poential Index. You can explore more about these indexs in the Housing Market Index page.

In the Detailed Info page, you can look at the existing AFTF projects in details.
Explore the housing indexs

You can explore the housing market of Louisville from three different perspectives: Initial Price Index, Price Change Index, and Affordable Housing Development Poential Index.

Initial price index represents the home sale price level in 2018. Price change index indicates the change level of home sale price in 5 years. Affordable housing development potential index shows the potential for new affordable housing projects in the future. All indexes are scaled from 0 to 10.
