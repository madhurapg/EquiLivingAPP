About the Website

The website will help identify neighborhoods at high risk of displacement and try to help the local government and NPOs to leverage their resources and finances when investing in these neighborhoods.

To identify displacement, this website works to show you the predicted home sale price of the city in the future. Choose the year you are interested in and see the predicted results. You can also define your own scenario for the city's future and see the predicted results based on what you anticipate.

Also, click specific areas to see detailed information of that area.
